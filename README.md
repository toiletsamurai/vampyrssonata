# vampyrssonata


**Genre:** Gothic/Steampunk Visual Novel  
**Engine:** Ren'Py  
**Status:** In development (placeholders & script in progress)  

---

## Overview
**Vampyr's Sonata** is a dark, gothic-steampunk visual novel set in a decaying nation cursed by an ancient sorcererâ€™s immortality spell. The game follows a young vampire hunter raised by an emotionally distant father after his mother was killed in a notorious vampire raid. Players navigate a world of rogue vampire aristocrats, criminal vampire factions, and dangerous secrets as they uncover the truth behind the curse and decide the fate of humans and vampires alike.

---

## Themes
- Vengeance vs morality  
- Humanity vs monstrosity  
- Generational trauma  
- Corruption of power  
- Forbidden love  
- Choices with consequences  

---

## Gameplay
- Branching narrative with multiple choices  
- Romance options (human and vampire routes)  
- 2D VN with cinematic 3D camera movement  
- Placeholder CGs for key story moments  
- Animated fight sequences (2D characters, 3D camera)  
- Multiple endings based on choices and relationships
